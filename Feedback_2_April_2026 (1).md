# Major Change

We will introduce a **role-based journey** to demonstrate the whole web app.

## Breakdown

So now, there are **4 pages**:

- Page 1 — Landing Page
- Page 2 — Admin
- Page 3 — Merchandiser
- Page 4 — Sample Locator

---

## Page 1: Landing Page

A first page where we see three options: **Admin**, **Merchandiser**, and **Sample Locator**.

When we click on each of the three pathways, the specific pathway opens.

The next 3 pages are **Admin**, **Merchandiser**, and **Sample Locator** each.

---

## Page 2: Admin *(Similar to current Dashboard page)*

- **Transfer**
- **Create Sample — Encode RFID**
- **Store**

> \*This is for all samples that arrive at Centro  
> \*This digitizes the pathway of the work done by the person sitting at the counter of the merchandiser floor

---

## Page 3: Merchandiser

Will show all the samples that came to the merchandiser and the samples that are in his store.

**Three actions available for each option (Receive):**

i. Accept  
ii. Transfer *(to another merchandiser / designer / shipped)*  
iii. Store  
iv. Received *(explained below)*

---

## Page 4: Sample Locator

*(Search samples with keywords)* — very similar to the current Storage View page.

Each sample will have two buttons:

- **Button 1 — Assign to me** *(if the sample is in storage)*
- **Button 2 — Request Transfer** *(if the sample is with a person)*

> **Note:** There will be a pop-up to the merchandiser who sent the request to transfer.  
> The pop-up will say: *Received — Yes or No?*
